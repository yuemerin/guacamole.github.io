RESPIRATORY SYSTEM

Function:

  • Facilitates the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the body
  • Oxygenates blood for delivery to tissues and removes carbon dioxide
  • Maintains acid-base balance through regulation of carbon dioxide levels

Components:

  • Upper respiratory tract: Nose, mouth, pharynx, larynx
  • Lower respiratory tract: Trachea, bronchi, bronchioles, alveoli

Mechanics of Breathing:

  • Inhalation: Diaphragm contracts, chest cavity expands, air is drawn into the lungs
  • Exhalation: Diaphragm relaxes, chest cavity contracts, air is expelled from the lungs

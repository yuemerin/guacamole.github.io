Function:

  • Circulates blood throughout the body to deliver oxygen, nutrients, and hormones
  • Removes waste products and carbon dioxide
  • Facilitates immune response and maintains homeostasis

Components:

  • Heart: Pump that propels blood through blood vessels
  • Blood vessels: Arteries, veins, and capillaries that form a network for blood flow
  • Blood: Contains red and white blood cells, platelets, and plasma
